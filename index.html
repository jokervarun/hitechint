About Hi-Tech International Trading

Established in 2008, Hi-Tech International Trading has proudly served Honda Road, Ruwi, Muscat, and all of Oman with top-quality building materials. We are dedicated to providing our customers—whether contractors, builders, or homeowners—with reliable products, exceptional service, and competitive prices. Our experienced team ensures timely deliveries and expert advice to help your projects succeed. At Hi-Tech International Trading, your satisfaction and trust are our greatest achievements.

Our 12,000 sq ft warehouse in Al‑Khuwair houses over 2,500 SKUs: from structural materials and finishing products to tools, safety gear and MEP supplies.
